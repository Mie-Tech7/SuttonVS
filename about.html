About the Studio

One founder.
Eight ventures.
One system.

Joshua Sutton

Founder · Chairman · Designer · Builder

Houston-based venture studio operator building AI-first platforms across music, civic tech, finance, cultural heritage, and custom apparel. Former MediaTech Ventures cohort member. Current Ion Incubator instructor.

The studio model: design and architect each platform, place operators and CEOs as ventures mature, retain strategic oversight as chairman across the portfolio.

Studio Philosophy

Patent-First

Intellectual property is the primary asset. Platforms are proof-of-concept. Licensing is the business model.

AI-Native by Default

Every build is designed for AI augmentation from the first commit — not bolted on after launch.

Operators Over Founders

The goal is to place the right CEO into each venture, not to run eight companies simultaneously.
